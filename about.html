About Us

We are pleased to introduce ourselves as a growing institute with NBA Accreditation in year 2016, ranked as 12th Best Engineering College in Telanagna State (By Times of India), All INDIA 8th Rank with Placements and All INDIA 26th Ranked Academically best Engineering College by HIGHER EDUCATIONAL REVIEW (New Delhi). St.Peter’s Engineering College, established in the year 2007 at Hyderabad (Telangana), is an AICTE approved “BMQR” ISO 9001 CERTIFIED Engineering Institute, affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Hyderabad (JNTUH). We are affiliated with Undergraduate courses in Computer Science & Engineering, Electronics and Communication Engineering, Electrical & Electronics Engineering, Mechanical Engineering and Civil Engineering and Post Graduate courses in Electrical and Electronics Engineering, Computer Science & Engineering and have a track record of outstanding performances of its pass-outs in different spheres. The entire campus is connected by a state-of-the-art network with 800 nodes.
